## Managing Your Billing Information  

Laxi.ai provides a dedicated Billing section where you can update your organization details, payment methods, and billing history. Keeping your billing information accurate ensures smooth transactions and access to your subscription.  

To access the Billing page, navigate to **Laxi.ai Dashboard → Billing**.  

<img src="/images/account/billing-tab.png" alt="Navigating to Billing Section in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '50%', marginLeft: 'auto', marginRight: 'auto' }} />  

---

## Organization Details  

To access Organization Details, go to **Billing → Organization Details**.  

<img src="/images/account/organization-tab.png" alt="Navigating to Organization Details" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

The Organization Details section allows you to manage essential billing-related information, including:  

- **Business Name & Address** – Enter your organization name, country, and full address.  
- **Tax Information** – Provide a tax type and tax identification number if applicable.  
- **Billing Contact** – Ensure your billing email is up to date to receive invoices and payment confirmations.  

<img src="/images/account/billing-details.png" alt="Billing details section in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

---

## Managing Payment Methods  

To access Payment Methods, go to **Billing → Payment Methods**.  

<img src="/images/account/payment-methods-tab.png" alt="Navigating to Payment Methods" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

In the Payment Methods section, you can store and manage the cards used for subscription payments.  

<img src="/images/account/payment-methods.png" alt="Managing payment methods in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

To add a new payment method:  

1. Click Add Payment Method.  
2. Enter your card details and save the information.  

<img src="/images/account/add-payment-method.png" alt="Adding a new payment method in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

<Tip>
Ensure at least one valid payment method is always active to avoid disruptions in your subscription.
</Tip>

## Viewing Billing History  

To access Billing History, go to **Billing → Billing History**.  

<img src="/images/account/billing-history-tab.png" alt="Navigating to Billing History" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

The Billing History section provides a complete record of past payments, including:  

- Transaction Dates  
- Amount Paid  
- Payment Status  
- Payment Method Used  
- Downloadable Invoices  

<img src="/images/account/billing-history.png" alt="Billing history in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />  

### Filtering & Exporting History  

You can filter records by selecting:  

- Last 3 months  
- Last 6 months  
- Last year  
- All time  

<img src="/images/account/billing-history-filter.png" alt="Filtering billing history in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '50%', marginLeft: 'auto', marginRight: 'auto' }} />  

To keep a local record, click Export History to download a complete invoice report.

Keeping your billing details updated ensures uninterrupted access to Laxi.ai's services. Regularly check your payment methods and transaction history to avoid any service disruptions.
