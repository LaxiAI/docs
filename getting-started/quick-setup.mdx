# Getting Started with Laxi.ai

Welcome to **Laxi.ai**! This comprehensive guide walks you through the essential steps to create and configure your AI-powered ecommerce assistant. Follow our structured approach to deploy a **fully functional chatbot** quickly and efficiently.

---

## 1. Account Setup & Authentication

Choose your preferred onboarding method:

### Shopify Merchants  
1. Navigate to our **[Shopify App Store listing](https://apps.shopify.com/laxi-ai)**.  
2. Select your store and click **Install**.  
3. Launch the app from your **Shopify admin panel**.  

<img src="/images/getting-started/shopify-app-install.png" alt="Shopify app installation interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

### WooCommerce & Custom Platforms  
1. Access our **[registration portal](https://laxi.ai/signup)**.  
2. Authenticate via **Google SSO or email verification**.  
3. Finalize **organizational profile details**.  

<img src="/images/getting-started/signup-form.png" alt="Account registration interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Note type="success">
Both authentication methods automatically provision your account and initiate dashboard access.
</Note>

## 2. Agent Initialization

1. From the **Laxi.ai dashboard**, select **Create New Agent**.  
2. Assign a unique identifier that reflects your **brand identity**.  
3. Configure your **ecommerce platform** integration (Shopify, WooCommerce, or Custom).  

<img src="/images/getting-started/create-new-agent.png" alt="Agent creation interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

Once created, your **new agent** will be listed in the deployment panel, ready for customization.
