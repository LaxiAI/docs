# Getting Started with Laxi.ai

Welcome to **Laxi.ai**! This comprehensive guide walks you through the essential steps to create and configure your AI-powered commerce assistant. Follow our structured approach to deploy a fully functional chatbot quickly and efficiently.

---

## 1. Account Setup & Authentication

Select your preferred onboarding method:

### Shopify Merchants
1. Navigate to our [Shopify App Store listing](https://apps.shopify.com/laxi-ai)
2. Select your store and click on "Install".
3. Launch the app from your Shopify admin panel

<img src="/images/getting-started/shopify-app-install.png" alt="Shopify app installation interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

### WooCommerce & Custom Platforms
1. Access our [registration portal](https://laxi.ai/signup)
2. Authenticate via Google SSO or email verification
3. Finalize organizational profile details

<img src="/images/getting-started/signup-form.png" alt="Account registration interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Note type="success">
Both authentication methods automatically provision your account and initiate dashboard access.
</Note>

## 2. Agent Initialization

1. From the control panel, select **Create New Agent**  
2. Assign a unique identifier reflecting your brand identity  
3. Configure your **ecommerce platform** integration (Shopify/WooCommerce/Custom)  

<img src="/images/getting-started/create-new-agent.png" alt="Agent creation interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

Your newly created agent will immediately appear in the deployment pipeline, ready for customization.

---

## 3. Core Configuration

### **3.1 Brand Identity Configuration**
Align the chatbot's visual presentation with your brand guidelines:
- Customize color schemes and typography  
- Define conversational tone and personality  
- Adjust UI component placement and dimensions  

<img src="/images/getting-started/customize.png" alt="Chatbot customization interface" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

> **Advanced customization:** Review our **[Style Guide](../agent-config/design-customizations)** for theme overrides and CSS injection.

### **3.2 Knowledge Base Integration**
Enhance your agent's knowledge base by integrating relevant data sources:

#### **Product Catalog Sync**
- Automated synchronization with:
  - Shopify product inventory
  - WooCommerce catalog
  - **Google Sheets** (CSV/Excel compatible)  

#### **Domain Expertise Upload**
- Augment AI capabilities with:
  - Policy documents (PDF/DOCX)
  - Website sitemap integration
  - Custom training datasets (Q&A format)  

<img src="/images/getting-started/data-sources.png" alt="Data source management console" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

> **Complete documentation:** Explore our **[Knowledge Base Management Guide](../agent-config/data-sources)**.
