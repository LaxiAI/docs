# Integrating Laxi.ai with WordPress

Laxi.ai integrates seamlessly with WordPress and WooCommerce, allowing you to add a fully functional AI chatbot to your online store. To complete the integration, you'll need to install and activate the **Laxi.ai for WooCommerce** plugin, then connect it to your Laxi account.

---

## 1. Installing the Laxi.ai Plugin

To begin the integration process, follow these steps:

1. Go to your WordPress Admin Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Plugins → Add New**.
3. Search for "Laxi AI for WooCommerce".
4. Click **Install Now**, then **Activate** once the installation is complete.

<img src="/images/integrations/wp-step1.png" alt="Installing Laxi.ai Plugin in WordPress" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Info>
    You can also download the plugin directly from the [WordPress Plugin Directory](https://wordpress.org/plugins/laxi-ai-for-woocommerce/) and upload it to your site manually.
</Info>

---

## 2. Activating and Connecting Your Store

After installation, you'll need to connect your WooCommerce store to your Laxi.ai account:

1. In your WordPress admin panel, locate and click on the **Laxi AI** menu item.
2. Click on **Connect Account** to begin the authentication process.
4. Grant permission for the plugin to access your store data.

<img src="/images/integrations/wp-step2.png" alt="Connecting your WooCommerce store to Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Tip>
    During the connection process, Laxi.ai will automatically sync your product catalog to provide accurate AI responses about your products.
</Tip>

## 4. Enabling the AI Agent on Your Site

The final step is to enable the AI Agent on your WooCommerce store:

1. In your WordPress admin, go to **Laxi AI**.
2. Toggle the **Active** switch to ON.

<img src="/images/integrations/wp-step3.png" alt="Enabling the Laxi.ai AI Agent on your WooCommerce store" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Warning>
    Make sure your theme is compatible with the latest version of WordPress and WooCommerce to ensure proper functionality of the Laxi.ai AI Agent.
</Warning>

---

## 5. Testing Your AI Agent

After enabling the AI Agent, visit your store's frontend to verify that it's working properly:

1. Open your WooCommerce store in a new browser tab.
2. Look for the Laxi.ai chat icon in the corner of your screen.
3. Click on the icon to open the chat interface.
4. Test the AI Agent by asking a few product-related questions.
