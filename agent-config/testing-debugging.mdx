## Debugging & Refining Responses  

If your chatbot gives **unexpected** or **incomplete** responses:  

- **Check your data sources** → Ensure all **products, FAQs, and documents** are correctly synced.  
- **Train your AI agent** → Click **Update AI Agent** after modifying knowledge sources.  
- **Refine chatbot settings** → Adjust the **tone, welcome messages, or suggested responses** in **Agent Configuration**.  

<Info>
Testing regularly helps maintain **accuracy and consistency** in customer interactions.
</Info>
