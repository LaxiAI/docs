# Data Sources  

Train your AI agent with knowledge from various sources to improve responses and recommendations.  

On the **Data Sources** page, you will find:  

- **Total Characters** used for training data.  
- **Total Products** synced with your AI.  
- **Last Updated Timestamp** indicating the most recent sync.  

---

## Integrate with Your Ecommerce Provider  

Select your preferred **integration method** to import product data.

### **For Shopify Users**  
If you are using Shopify, your store’s product catalog is **automatically connected**.  

<Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" /> Your **products sync automatically** with Laxi.ai.  
<Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" /> You **cannot remove** Shopify as a data source, but you can **resync anytime**.  

**To resync Shopify data:** Click the resync icon to pull the latest updates.

<img src="/images/agent-config/shopify-sync.png" alt="Resync Shopify data with Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Info>
Since Shopify is directly linked, **this integration cannot be removed**, but you can **resync anytime**.
</Info>

---

### **For Non Shopify Users**  
If you are **not using Shopify**, you can **connect your WooCommerce store manually**.

#### **Connect a WooCommerce Store**  
1. Enter your **Store URL**.  
2. Provide your **Consumer Key & Consumer Secret**.  
3. Click **Connect** to sync product data.  

<img src="/images/agent-config/woocommerce-sync.png" alt="Connect WooCommerce store to Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

## **Manually Upload Product Data (Google Sheets)**  
This option is available for **both Shopify and non-Shopify users**.  

1. Download the **[Google Sheets Template](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1guASJrK34cW_Ll8qZQBSYsVJtuR_NLKAroz3On4E9sc/edit?usp=sharing)** to ensure proper formatting.  
2. Fill in product details according to the template.  
3. Upload the file in the **Data Sources** section.  

<img src="/images/agent-config/google-sheets.png" alt="Google Sheets product import" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

## Add Company Information  

Laxi.ai allows you to **enrich your AI agent** with additional business-related data.  

<Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" /> **Upload Files** – PDF, DOCX, or text files.  
<Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" /> **Add URLs** – Link relevant company pages.  
<Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" /> **Define FAQs** – Provide common customer queries and responses.  

### **Upload Knowledge Documents**  
1. Click **Upload File** or drag-and-drop a document.  
2. Supported formats: **PDF, DOCX, TXT**.  
3. The AI will process the file to extract relevant data.  

<img src="/images/agent-config/file-upload.png" alt="Upload company knowledge files" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

## Update AI Agent  

Once your data is synced, you need to **update the AI agent** to ensure it is trained on the latest information.

Click **Update AI Agent** to apply the newly synced data and improve chatbot accuracy.

<img src="/images/agent-config/update-ai-agent.png" alt="Update AI Agent in Laxi.ai" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Tip>
To keep your AI agent performing at its best, regularly **update the AI agent** after syncing data. This ensures it reflects the **latest product catalog, FAQs, and company knowledge** for more accurate responses.
</Tip>
